About Us

Why Our Clients Choose Us

NT Nails understands that Safety and Sanitation are key to your peace of mind. Our implements are medically sterilized and disinfected after each use. Buffers and files are used only once then discarded. All pedicure procedures are done with liner protection. And above all, staff are trained to follow a proper sanitization protocol that puts client's hygiene as the number one priority.

Visit us today and experience the combination of luxury and elegance. Our nail salon looks forward to seeing you!
